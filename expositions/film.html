In nomine ludi.

Illuminated by the moonlight, Martin is seen fiddling at the main entrance door of a church. With just a few skillful moves, he breaks into the church.

"In nomine ludi" - "In the name of the game" breaks down the values and mission of the church in the simplest possible way, as if the whole thing were just a game.

Production, conceptualization, direction, cut and costumes for the short movie. 6 min. Produced in February 2022 in a tiny church in Elsbethen, Austria.
