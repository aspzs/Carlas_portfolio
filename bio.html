    • ◌ about

    • Carla Schwering was born on March 31, 1997 in Vienna, Austria. After her double high-school degree at the German-French-school in Munich, Germany in 2015 and several internships, she enrolled in theatre studies and art history (BA) at the LMU in 2016.

    • ◌ me

    • Carla has always had an interest in photography and film, as they can be used independently as well as in the context of theatre. She is particularly fascinated by how the media can be used creatively: for example changing photos afterwards and intervening in them, like on stage or in a film set, where you transform the real space and consciously use the dream and fiction. But she is also fascinated by drawings and paintings and how compositions and colors are able to express so much.
      Carla likes to approach her projects theoretically on a base of research, to then conceptually choose the right medium and find a metaphorical and poetical access and translation to what she wants to express. Her work is mainly based on already existing material, which she recontextualizes in a way to find more of a personal path and to give it a relevant contribution to contemporary society.
      She likes themes like dream, antique myths, psychological theories, literature, etc. An idea that fascinates her is the concept of < authenticity > in our society, and how we translate our worls with authentic artistic expression.
